My Approach

My Philosophy

I believe in bringing operational rigor to talent acquisition without making it feel transactional. Recruitment shouldn't rely on gut feelings or manual processes that slow everyone down. I focus on tech-enabled workflows, using modern ATS platforms, Salesforce, and AI-powered tools to eliminate friction so recruiters can spend their time building relationships, not managing spreadsheets.

Focusing on What Matters

Hiring fast doesn't mean much if people don't stay. I focus heavily on Quality-of-Hire, tracking how people actually perform and fit into culture quarters and years down the road. By combining clear data with real human insight, we build hiring processes that don't just fill open seats, but genuinely strengthen the company.

My Framework

Iterative TA Maturity Model

Most recruiting functions get stuck in reactive mode. This is the framework I use to move organizations from demand-driven hiring to a sustainable, intelligence-led sourcing engine, in three deliberate stages.

01
BUILD
Establish the Foundation

Reactive hiring gives way to intentional process. The goal is to establish clear ownership, baseline infrastructure, and visibility into what's actually happening.

  • Sourcing ModelDemand-driven; managed reactively by team lead
  • ProcessBasic screen-and-submit workflow; demand requests come from hiring leaders
  • TechnologyCore ATS configured; foundational reporting and requisition tracking in place
  • AI ToolsInitial exploration of AI for job description writing and basic candidate matching
  • OutreachRecruiter-led outreach; messaging is ad hoc and inconsistent across the team
  • MarketingMinimal partnership; employer brand largely undefined
  • Vendor UseHigh; external agencies fill sourcing gaps
02
OPTIMIZE
Refine & Systematize

With a foundation in place, the focus shifts to closing gaps and making sourcing proactive rather than reactive.

  • Sourcing ModelAgile model; dedicated point of contact per hiring leader and team
  • ProcessStreamlined screen, interview, and offer experience with active feedback loops
  • TechnologyATS fully utilized; real-time reporting and pipeline dashboards live for hiring managers
  • AI ToolsAI used for outreach personalization, interview scheduling, and sourcing workflow automation
  • OutreachTemplated messaging aligned to role and persona; tested and refined by channel
  • MarketingActive collaboration on employer brand campaigns; recruiting and marketing share content calendar
  • Vendor UseReduced; internal sourcing handles the majority of requisitions
03
SCALE
Sustain & Evolve

A mature, sustainable sourcing engine that runs with or without constant oversight. Data drives decisions; the team is strategic, not just transactional.

  • Sourcing ModelProactive, intelligence-led; passive talent pipeline always active
  • ProcessFirmwide consistency; continuous improvement cycles built in
  • TechnologyATS integrated across the full talent lifecycle; automation reduces manual work firmwide
  • AI ToolsAI embedded across sourcing, screening, and reporting; team focuses on relationships and judgment calls
  • OutreachBrand-consistent, personalized outreach at scale; voice shaped in partnership with marketing
  • MarketingRecruiting is a brand function; talent team contributes to EVP, social strategy, and candidate experience
  • Vendor UseMinimal; reserved for niche or surge scenarios only
